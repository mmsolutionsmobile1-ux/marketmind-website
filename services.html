Specimen Collection

Trained and certified field staff perform specimen collections following strict safety, labeling, and chain-of-custody protocols. Every collection is traceable, documented, and handled to lab-grade standards.

  • Certified staff only
  • Immediate specimen labeling
  • Temperature-controlled storage
  • Valid provider order required

Non-Negotiables

Three rules. No exceptions.

These are not guidelines — they are the foundation of how we operate. Every site. Every day.

No Order, No Test
A licensed provider must order testing before any specimen is collected. There is no workaround, no exception, and no gray area on this rule.
No Insurance, No Visit
Active coverage must be confirmed before any service begins. Every patient's eligibility is verified in real time, every single visit.
No ID, No Encounter
Patient identity must be verified with a valid, government-issued photo ID before anything else happens. No ID means no encounter — full stop.
