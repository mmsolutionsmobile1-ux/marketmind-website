The Role

Your role on the ground

Site managers are the backbone of our operations. Here's what day-to-day ownership looks like.

01 — Operations
Oversee Daily Operations
Manage the day-to-day flow of the site, ensure staff are in place, and keep everything running on schedule and on standard from open to close.
02 — Team
Manage Staff & Workflow
Coordinate your team, assign responsibilities, and maintain a professional, organized environment. Good leadership is visible in how a site runs, not just what it produces.
03 — Compliance
Maintain Compliance
Follow all identity verification, insurance confirmation, specimen handling, and documentation procedures without exception. This is non-negotiable and central to the role.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to have a team already?
Not required, but preferred. Solo managers with strong operational experience are also considered. If you have a team ready to deploy, lead with that — it's a significant advantage.
Is this full-time or part-time?
These are contract / independent contractor opportunities. Hours depend on site volume and location. Some engagements run full-time equivalent, others are more flexible.
What kind of experience do I need?
Prior experience running testing sites, mobile clinics, or specimen collection operations is strongly preferred. If you've managed multi-staff sites and dealt with compliance requirements, you're the kind of person we want to talk to.
Where are locations available?
Multiple opportunities are available across different areas. Contact us directly for details on current openings in your region — we'll let you know what's available near you.
How do I stand out in my application?
Be specific about your past experience — the sites you've managed, the size of teams you've run, and how you've handled compliance or operational challenges. Vague applications get passed over. Concrete experience gets attention.
