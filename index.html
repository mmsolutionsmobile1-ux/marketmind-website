What We Do

A smarter way to reach
patients in the field

From site setup to telehealth integration, we handle every layer of mobile clinic operations — so every patient encounter is safe, documented, and compliant.

🏥
Mobile Site Operations
Full site setup, staff coordination, and daily operations management. We design workflows that keep every location running without friction.
🔬
Specimen Collection
Trained, certified field staff perform collections following strict safety, labeling, and chain-of-custody protocols — every time.
💻
Telehealth Integration
Every patient encounter connects to a licensed provider through our partner platform. Testing only happens when a provider orders it.
🛡️
Compliance & Documentation
A formal compliance program governs every aspect of our operations — from identity verification to incident reporting and audit trails.
🌍
Community Outreach

📋
Staff Training & Support

How It Works

From first contact to results —
every step covered

Our process is built to protect patients and staff at every touchpoint, with no shortcuts and no gaps.

1
Patient Arrives
Comes to the mobile site with insurance card and valid photo ID in hand.
2
Verify & Check In
Staff verifies ID, confirms active insurance eligibility, and collects vitals.
3
Telehealth Consult
A licensed provider conducts the consultation and determines whether testing is needed.
4
Testing & Results
Specimen collected only if ordered, sent to the lab, and results delivered to the provider.
